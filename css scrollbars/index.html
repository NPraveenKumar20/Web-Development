Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational technology company that offers
business consulting, information technology, and outsourcing services.
Founded in 1981, the company is headquartered in Bengaluru. On 24 August 2021, Infosys became the fourth Indian company to achieve a market capitalization
of US$100 billion. As of 2024, Infosys is the second-largest Indian Big Tech company
by revenue and market capitalization.
